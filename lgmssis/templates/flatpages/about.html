About Us

LGMS is duly recognized by the Department of Education in 2003 for our Casa course and 2006 for complete elementary course.

Who we are

LGMS is a non-stock, non-profit, non- sectarian family owned educational institution, which started January of 2000.The school started with a small group of children on a rented bungalow house in Aguirre exactly where our small building now is situated.

As a continuous service for the students and parents, LGMS purchased the house and lot , demolished the bungalow house and re-built a new building in 2005 to cater to the growing population . From then on, yearly renovations and building improvements are being done.

What is Montessori

The Montessori approach offers a broad vision of education as an aid to life.

The method caters to the needs of the individual, regardless of the level of ability, learning style and maturity.
