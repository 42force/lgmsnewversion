COURSE DESCRIPTION

LGMS classrooms are designed for a 3 year age mix from 2 ½ to 6 years old. It provides a prepared environment where Children are free to respond to their natural drive to work and learn. Children are given opportunitiesn to engage in spontaneous meaningful activities under the guidance of a trained adult. Through their work, the children develop concentration, order, discipline, independence and love of work.
